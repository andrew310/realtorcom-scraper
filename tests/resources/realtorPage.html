About Diane Hermis Schrakamp

Agents: Edit this profile 

I have lived in Houston all of my life and have been a real estate agent since 2004. I acquired my brokers license in 2015 and now I am Broker/Owner of Total Real Estate Services. I am a very detail oriented person and make sure that my tasks are done correctly the first time in order to streamline the closing process. I am an excellent negotiator with 14 years of experience under my belt and I know a lot of tips and tricks to help you buy or sell your home for a great price! If you are looking for an extremely hard working, honest competent, and detail oriented agent to work for you, I am your agent. I can say without a doubt that you will NOT be disappointed in my service!

In my spare time I enjoy traveling to new and exciting places with my husband and playing with our two doggies. I also volunteer for a dog rescue here in Houston.
